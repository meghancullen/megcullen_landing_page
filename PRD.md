# **Product Requirement Document**

## **01 — Problem Statement**

Meghan Cullen is a **Business Analytics and Marketing** senior at the **University of Iowa** (graduating **May 2026**). While a traditional resume passes through ATS filters, it fails to capture Meghan's "coachable" mindset, her drive to improve team dynamics, and the visual evidence of her analytical projects. This landing page solves the problem of "static representation" by providing a dynamic, branded space that proves her technical proficiency and leadership potential to prospective employers.

## **02 — Target Audience**

The primary audience consists of **recruiters and hiring managers** at **large global firms** (mirroring her international experience in Germany) and **mid-sized marketing agencies** in the Midwest and beyond. These visitors are looking for entry-level talent who possess a mix of data-driven decision-making skills and a proactive, learning-oriented attitude.

## **03 — Goals & Success Metrics**

| Goal | How We Measure It |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Increase Professional Visibility** | Number of unique site visits over the first 30 days. |
| **Drive Recruitment Engagement** | Number of clicks on the LinkedIn or Email contact links. |
| **Validate Technical Brand** | At least 2 direct inquiries or messages referencing specific projects found on the site. |
